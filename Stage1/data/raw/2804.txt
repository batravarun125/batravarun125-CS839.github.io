The beloved celebrity makeup artist Charlotte Tilbury’s new collection of lipsticks — in glowy shades from pale nude to retro coral to rich mulberry — are each named after a different woman she works with, from Helena Bonham Carter to Nicole Kidman, and each come with a square, angled tip for the most precise application.
